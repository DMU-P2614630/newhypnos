Our Website

Hypnos is a mobile application designed for the user who suffers from poor sleep quality. This application offers personalized relaxing sounds and meditation guides before sleeping. Relaxing sounds include natural sounds, white noise and other electronic sounds with the right frequency. Our Goal

This application was created to focus on helping users sleep more efficiently because most people spend 8 hours of sleep per day on average, about 1/3 of the day each day. But some may be less. For some reason, people have stress from work and they do not have enough time to sleep and help people calm down and enjoy their sleep.They can be focused on work daily without being lethargic. The user can choose the color according to their mood and manage the white noise that they want to listen to. The aim of the application is to help the user to explore their sleep quality and help them to sleep well each night.
